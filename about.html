Being fast and efficient

We are two engineers with combined 40+ years of experience in the field of particle accelerators. This unique experience we have bundled in the design of a state of the art control system (Blinky-Lite) which satisfied our needs and supports us in our mission to work highly efficient.

We use this full-stack control system now to develop dedicated solutions for our customers, either to integrate sophisticated particle accelerating structures or for any supporting systems thereof (e.g. leak detection systems, etc).

Due to its full-stack nature, every system we build can fully live on its own or can be in exchange with an existing control system through a RESTful API interface.

We start at the sensor, design two layered PCB electronic hardware interfaces where necessary and provide the fully integrated system to our customers via a dedicated web aplication that is securely accessible through two factor authentification (2FA).

Utilising secure, wireless communication technology, Blinky-Lite also enables us to add systems to already existing facility installations without the need to pull new control cables.

Installations at our customers around the globe have proven to be highly reliable over the years and meanwhile we can refer to installations at renowned research facilities in Sweden (MAX IV), the US (Fermilab) and in Hungary (Mirrotron). Live system examples thereof can be found at this link.

Since the construction of particle accelerators is already a challenging endeavour due to the manufacturing complexity of very demanding components, our vision is to accelerate the commercialization of (compact) particle accelerators through fully integrated, ready to go subsystems.
